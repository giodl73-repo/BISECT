# Pulse 06 -- Close Or Promote

## Goal

Decide whether Track Y remains a research-only weighting experiment, continues
to a full CA-52 research run, or opens a follow-up Layer 1 structure wave.

## Decision Options

### Option A -- Close As Research-Only

Use if:

- synthetic behavior is weak or ambiguous;
- subset evidence does not improve interpretability;
- cohesion weights add complexity without clearer cut evidence.

Disposition:

- keep Track Y docs;
- mark `--weights-override cohesion` deferred or experimental only;
- do not run full CA-52.

### Option B -- Continue To CA-52 Research Run

Use if:

- synthetic fixtures pass;
- California subset evidence is interpretable;
- cohesion sidecar explains changed cut choices;
- no forbidden field access or invariance failures appear.

Disposition:

- add a new pulse or wave for CA-52 research only;
- compare against `prime-factor + geographic`;
- keep public wording as research evidence.

### Option C -- Open Layer 1 Follow-Up

Use only if:

- Layer 2 repeatedly reveals stable cohesion modules;
- pre-factoring district counts to modules has a clear reason;
- synthetic and subset evidence show Layer 2 is insufficient.

Disposition:

```text
--structure cohesion-factor
```

must open as a new wave, not as scope creep in Y.1.
