## Claims
1. The 2030 Census data will be released April 2031. Congressional redistricting must complete in most states by late 2031 — a window of 6-12 months.
2. The bisect pipeline's data infrastructure (block-group adjacency for 39+ states, block adjacency for 3 states) requires 12-18 months to pre-build and validate. Infrastructure preparation should begin by 2029.
3. Four pipeline preparation tasks for 2030 are identified: (a) pre-build adjacency graphs at block-group resolution for all states, (b) update LODES and ACS data to 2028 vintage, (c) validate demographic projections against actuals as they arrive, (d) pre-register the algorithm version to be used.
4. The DIA's statutory seed formula (SHA-256 of Census release identifier) is designed to prevent last-minute algorithm changes: the seed is determined only after the Census data is released, preventing pre-optimization.
