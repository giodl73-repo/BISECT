# Requirements

## RQ-00 Scope

Repo or feature: BISECT / apportionment workspace at `C:\src\apportionment`.

These requirements are accepted from the locked mission IDs in `docs/vtrace/MISSION.md` and CONOPS scenario IDs in `docs/vtrace/CONOPS.md`. Requirement IDs are locked after role review.

First-class controlled interfaces for this VTRACE baseline are BISECT configs/CLI surfaces, RPLAN packages, RCOUNT packages, RCTX context/crosswalk packages, RHIST unit-history packages, public verification artifacts, research claim/evidence records, and wave/pulse execution records.
