\section{Introduction}

U.14 is a methodology paper about choosing among trade-offs. It treats
multi-objective outputs as decision surfaces that require explicit selection
rationale and audit lineage.

The paper extends U.12 and U.13 into the selection setting. A Pareto frontier,
ensemble cloud, or SMC sample can reveal alternatives; it does not choose among
them without a rule. The selection rule may be technical, policy-driven, or
administrative, but it must be recorded because it changes the claim attached
to the exported plan.
