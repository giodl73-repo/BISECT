\section{Audit Artifacts}

Selected plans should export objective vectors, candidate lineage, selection
parameters, and final assignments through the RPLAN/RCTX fixed point. Audit
checks verify declared artifacts, not the normative correctness of the chosen
trade-off.

The audit package should include enough metadata to replay the decision at the
artifact level: candidate identifiers, objective values, dominance or scoring
rule, tie breaks, and the final selected RPLAN. This creates a reviewable
record even when the selection rule itself is contested.
