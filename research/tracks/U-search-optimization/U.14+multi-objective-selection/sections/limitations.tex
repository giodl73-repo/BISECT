\section{Limitations}

Multi-objective selection can expose trade-offs but cannot decide legal or
political values by itself. Empirical claims require recorded candidate sets,
commands, and objective computations.

The current draft is methodological. Publication readiness requires concrete
examples from exported-plan, ensemble, SMC, or Pareto workflows, including the
commands that produced candidate sets and the sidecars that preserved objective
vectors. Until then, claims should remain about audit discipline rather than
measured selection performance.
