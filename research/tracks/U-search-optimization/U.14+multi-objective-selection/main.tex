\begin{abstract}
This draft frames selection among objective trade-offs after construction,
ensemble sampling, SMC, or Pareto search. It emphasizes how selected plans
should carry objective vectors, selection rationale, and RPLAN/RCTX audit
lineage without overclaiming neutrality. A selected plan is treated as a
decision record: candidate set, objective definition, selection rule, and final
audit package must travel together.
\end{abstract}
