\begin{abstract}
This paper presents RPLAN, RCTX, audit certificates, manifests, and verifier
integration as the BISECT fixed point for heterogeneous redistricting
algorithms. It is an audit/certification infrastructure paper: certificates
verify declared profiles, canonical ordering, hashes, package integrity, and
lineage. They do not certify political fairness, legal sufficiency, or
optimality beyond the recorded profile and attached evidence.
\end{abstract}
