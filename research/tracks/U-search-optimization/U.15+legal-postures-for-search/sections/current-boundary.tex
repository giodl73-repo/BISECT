\section{Current Implementation Boundary}

The legal posture of a search result depends on the artifact class.  A seed
ledger supports reproducibility; an ensemble summary supports a comparison
under declared diagnostics; a solver certificate supports a model-scoped bound;
and an RPLAN/RCTX audit package supports verifier replay of declared plan
properties.  None of these artifacts alone proves statewide legal sufficiency.

The current U.16--U.20 papers provide the stronger implementation reference
points.  Older posture examples should be read as argument templates that must
be backed by those package and certificate forms before they are used in
commission or court-facing settings.
