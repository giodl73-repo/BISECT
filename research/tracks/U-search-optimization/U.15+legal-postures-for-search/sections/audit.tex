\section{Audit Artifacts}

RPLAN/RCTX artifacts can show what was generated, selected, verified, and
reported. They cannot by themselves establish constitutional compliance,
statutory adequacy, or the absence of discriminatory effect.

For legal-facing use, the audit package should be paired with a claim memo that
names the posture, evidence level, artifact set, omitted evidence, and
jurisdiction-specific assumptions. The algorithmic record is an input to that
memo, not the memo itself.
