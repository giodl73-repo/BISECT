\begin{abstract}
This draft defines a practitioner-facing matrix for choosing among construction,
search, exact optimization, ensemble, Pareto, and audit paths. The central
claim is methodological: algorithm choice should follow the claim type, data
availability, required certificate, and RPLAN/RCTX audit surface.
\end{abstract}
