\section{Conclusion}

U.12 turns the algorithm-family roadmap into an operational choice table:
select the method family that matches the claim, then carry its output through
the declared audit path.

This keeps the family usable as it grows. New methods can be added by stating
their claim type, evidence level, CLI surface, and audit handoff rather than by
being forced into a single leaderboard.

The intended fixed point is simple: every algorithmic result should arrive with
the smallest honest claim it can support and the artifacts another reader needs
to check that claim.
