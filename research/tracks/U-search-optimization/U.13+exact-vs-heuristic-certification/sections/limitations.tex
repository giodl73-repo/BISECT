\section{Limitations}

Certificates are always scoped to a model, dataset, objective, and profile.
The paper must avoid implying that exactness for one mathematical formulation
settles broader legal, representational, or political questions.

The current implementation papers are staged at different evidence levels.
Until U.16 and U.17 document solver contracts and fixtures, exact certificates
should be described as target contracts. Until U.18 and U.19 document empirical
protocols, heuristic claims should be described as validity and comparison
claims rather than dominance claims.
