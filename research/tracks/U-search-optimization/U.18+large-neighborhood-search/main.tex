\begin{abstract}
This paper documents the BISECT local-search and large-neighborhood-search
improvement contract. It treats \texttt{bisect improve} as a
lineage-preserving heuristic implementation: improved plans, rejected moves,
no-op outcomes, and staged LNS/tabu paths must all produce reproducible
summaries. Accepted improvements must produce RPLAN/RCTX descendants that name
the parent plan, child artifact, method, status, objective delta, and
validation profile.
\end{abstract}
