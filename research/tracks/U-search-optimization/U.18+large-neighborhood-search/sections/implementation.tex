\section{Implementation Contract}

The current implementation centers on a one-move boundary improvement baseline
plus structured statuses for no-improvement and staged-method paths. The
summary must record parent plan id, attempted method, accepted move or no-op
reason, objective delta, validity checks, and output artifact id.
