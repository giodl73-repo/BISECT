\begin{abstract}
This paper documents the BISECT evolutionary and Pareto comparison path. It
separates frontier quality from selected-plan validity and requires fixed-seed
metadata, validity-preserving operators, and RPLAN/RCTX audit packages for any
selected frontier entry. A non-dominated entry is treated as an observed search
result under a declared budget, not as a legal or policy preference by itself.
\end{abstract}
