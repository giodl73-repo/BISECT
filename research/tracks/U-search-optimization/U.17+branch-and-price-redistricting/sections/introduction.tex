\section{Introduction}

U.17 documents \texttt{bisect-column} and \texttt{bisect-cli::exact\_cmd},
exposed as \texttt{bisect exact --method branch-and-price}. The paper is an
exact-optimization implementation paper about column-generation contracts, not
a claim that the large-instance pricing strategy is complete.

The purpose is to make branch-and-price inspectable before production-scale
solver integration. U.16 works with assignment variables and connectivity cuts;
U.17 works with whole district columns and a set-partitioning master problem.
U.20 then gives both families the same fixed-point destination: selected plans
become RPLAN/RCTX artifacts, while solver-specific evidence stays in the
sidecar reports and method lineage.
