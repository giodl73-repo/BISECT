\section{Conclusion}

Canvass arithmetic is the discipline of replaying public count evolution without
collapsing it into a single final table. RCOUNT represents status-specific
snapshots, jurisdiction rollups, status events, batch manifests, and source
hashes so that changes can be explained and failures can be attributed to the
right layer. The \texttt{canvass-correction}, \texttt{mail-batch-added}, and
\texttt{missing-batch} fixtures are small, but they establish the core contract:
changed totals need public lineage, batches need accounting evidence, and
mechanical verification must stop short of legal certification.
