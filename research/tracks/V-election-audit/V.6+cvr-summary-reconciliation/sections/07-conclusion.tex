\section{Conclusion}

CVR-to-summary reconciliation is where public verification moves from totals to
interpreted ballot evidence. The implemented V.6 slice reconciles normalized
CVR contest rows to public summaries and keeps the summary-side invariant in
place: selections plus residuals must equal counted ballots, and bad local
evidence must not hide inside jurisdiction totals.

That gives V.7 a stable base for replayable risk-limiting audits, while keeping
the remaining CVR privacy and vendor-adapter questions visible instead of
pretending they are already finished.
