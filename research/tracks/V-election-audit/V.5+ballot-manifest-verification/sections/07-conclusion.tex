\section{Conclusion}

Ballot manifest verification is the accounting floor under public totals. A
late mail batch can be legitimate when it is represented by manifest evidence,
status events, source references, and reconciled summaries. A summary that
points at a missing batch is a mechanical failure.

That distinction is what RCOUNT is for: not to replace canvass judgment, but to
make the public evidence behind canvass totals replayable and falsifiable.
