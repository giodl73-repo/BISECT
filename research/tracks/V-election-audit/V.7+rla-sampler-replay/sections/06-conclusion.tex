\section{Conclusion}

RCOUNT now has an implemented replay contract for the first half of an RLA:
the sample list. The verifier recomputes the contest population hash and every
draw from public seed material. A passing transcript says the sample list is
not merely asserted; it is replayable.

The remaining V.7 work is the statistical stopping layer. With sampler replay
in place, that layer can focus on observations, discrepancies, margins, and
escalation without also carrying the burden of proving how the sample was
chosen.
