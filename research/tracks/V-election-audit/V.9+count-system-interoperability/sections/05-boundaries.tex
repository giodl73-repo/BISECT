\section{Boundaries}

The statement CSV adapter does not certify an election. It does not prove that a
voting system recorded voter intent correctly, that voters were eligible, that
every ballot was lawfully accepted, or that a jurisdiction's official board has
completed its statutory duties. It only proves that a source file was preserved,
that understood fields were normalized into RCOUNT records, and that the
resulting package passes the mechanical RCOUNT equations.

The adapter also does not claim to be a complete NIST Election Results Reporting
implementation. Its purpose is to establish the RCOUNT import pattern before
larger CDF and vendor-specific adapters arrive.
