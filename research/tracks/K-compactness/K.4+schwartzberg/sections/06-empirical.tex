corresponding to mean PP $= 1/1.74^2 \approx 0.33$.
The maximum district S under ratio-optimal is $2.00$, meaning all 8 CO districts
satisfy S $\leq 2.0$ (equivalent to PP $\geq 0.25$).
This is a strong statutory compliance result for any jurisdiction that mandates
S $\leq 2.0$.
