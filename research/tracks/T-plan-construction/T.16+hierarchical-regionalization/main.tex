\begin{abstract}
This draft presents T.16, a hierarchical regionalization baseline for BISECT.
The paper focuses on deterministic agglomeration, merge witnesses, capacity and
contiguity checks, and RPLAN-compatible lineage. Regionalization is framed as a
construction family that makes the sequence of region merges inspectable, not
as proof that the merge sequence is optimal, legally sufficient, or politically
fair.
\end{abstract}
