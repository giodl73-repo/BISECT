\section{Limitations}

The current implementation is a deterministic regionalization baseline, not a
complete SKATER or Max-p reproduction. Merge scoring choices can affect output
quality and should be evaluated empirically before broad claims.

Merge lineage improves inspectability but does not make the merge sequence
normatively correct. Connectedness and capacity checks do not imply compactness,
community preservation, partisan fairness, or legal compliance beyond the
declared audit profile.
