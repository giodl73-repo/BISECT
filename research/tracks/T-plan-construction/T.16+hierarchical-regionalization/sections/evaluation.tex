\section{Evaluation Plan}

The current evidence layer includes path, grid, two-clique, capacity, and
determinism fixtures, plus a benchmark-tier RPLAN package generated from the
regionalization constructor on a 100-unit synthetic path. The next evidence
layer should compare regionalization against flat clustering, METIS, and flow
construction on selected real-data smoke states.

The current evidence should be read as implementation and packaging evidence:
the constructor produces deterministic merge logs, summaries, and verifier-facing
packages on fixtures. It is not yet evidence that regionalization produces
better compactness, fewer subdivision splits, or more persuasive community
outcomes than flat clustering, METIS, spectral partitioning, or flow
construction.
