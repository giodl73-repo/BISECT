\section{Evaluation Plan}

The current evidence layer includes deterministic seed tests, infeasible
capacity fixtures, connectedness checks, RPLAN audit package tests, and a
benchmark-tier RPLAN package generated from the capacity constructor on a
100-unit synthetic path. The next empirical layer should measure feasibility
and quality on selected real-data smoke states.

The comparison target is not just score quality. T.15 should be judged by
whether it explains capacity pressure better than alternatives: which units
could not be assigned, whether repair changed the plan, and what verifier
evidence exists after export. Quality metrics such as compactness, subdivision
splits, and partisan measures remain future empirical comparisons until the
same states and tolerances are run across the construction portfolio.
