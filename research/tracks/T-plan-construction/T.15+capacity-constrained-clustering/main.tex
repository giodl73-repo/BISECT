\begin{abstract}
This draft introduces T.15, a capacity-constrained clustering construction
family for BISECT. The implementation emphasizes deterministic seeding,
population-capacity status, repair-aware summaries, and RPLAN audit sidecars.
The paper frames clustering as a construction alternative with explicit
failure and repair states, not as a claim of global optimality.
\end{abstract}
