# Audition Pool

## Editorial Question

The issue should answer a planning question, not merely a forecasting question:
what must be built, validated, and published before the 2031 redistricting
window opens?

## Audition Recommendation

Proceed with a five-piece preview that follows the track order:

1. Editorial on why 2030 matters now.
2. Demographic projections and boundary states.
3. Sun Belt and Rust Belt consequences for algorithm design.
4. Data infrastructure requirements by state and resolution level.
5. Reapportionment projections and algorithmic stability.

Do not turn the volume into a legal or reform-position issue. Its job is to
prepare the system for the next cycle.
