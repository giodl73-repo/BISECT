## Reader Promise (expanded)

2030 redistricting begins when the Census releases usable data after the count. States typically have compressed windows to complete their maps. Algorithmic redistricting infrastructure -- adjacency graphs, validated bisection trees, published versions, and pre-registration rules -- needs to be ready before that window opens. Meanwhile, demographic shifts may reshape the House map. This volume makes the planning case without treating projections as settled facts.
