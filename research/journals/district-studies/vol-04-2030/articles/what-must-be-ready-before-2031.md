# What Must Be Ready Before 2031

The closing article should make the planning lesson explicit. Before 2031,
states need a projection source, a validated infrastructure plan, a resolution
strategy, and a publication workflow that can survive the compressed redistricting
window.

This is the issue's guardrail. It should leave the reader with a concrete
planning checklist, not a final opinion about the 2030 map.

If the earlier articles do their work, this one can stay short. Its purpose is to
prevent the volume from sounding like it has solved the projection problem when
it has only organized it.
