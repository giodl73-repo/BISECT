# 2030 Reapportionment Projections

The projection question is not whether the 2030 seat map changes. It does. The
question is how to present that change without confusing a projection with a
final apportionment.

This article should frame the projected gainers, losers, and boundary states as
planning inputs. It should also explain why stability analysis matters: the map
construction pipeline must know where the pressure points are before the census
data arrives.

The clean public line is that the projection helps operators prepare. It does
not decide the map.
