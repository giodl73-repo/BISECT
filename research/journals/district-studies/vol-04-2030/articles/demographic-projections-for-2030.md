# Demographic Projections for 2030

Projected population change is the first thing that makes the 2030 cycle
different from 2020. The projected seat map moves toward the Sun Belt, with the
boundary states carrying the most uncertainty.

The public version should make two things clear. First, the projection series is
not a settled result. Second, the boundary states are the ones that need the
most attention because they sit closest to the seat-change threshold.

Readers do not need the full mechanics of apportionment to understand the
planning implication: if a state is near a boundary, infrastructure and staffing
decisions should assume that the projected allocation could move.
