# Audition Pool

## Editorial Question

The issue should not ask readers to accept a new population rule at the start. It should first show that "population equality" hides a definitional choice, then ask which definitions are administratively, legally, and empirically defensible.

## Audition Recommendation

Proceed with a five-piece preview only if the issue remains qualitative:

1. An editorial on why counting is a choice.
2. A prison-gerrymandering lead article using N.1, stripped of litigation conclusions.
3. A taxonomy article from N.0.
4. A citizen-VAP caution article from N.3, without the exact national seat-shift estimate.
5. A synthesis/guardrail article from N.5 explaining why the recommendation is not locked.
