# Who Counts?

> Provisional public preview. This issue is a staged population-base draft, not
> a locked publication.

Population equality sounds simple until the population base is named. This issue
asks which population a redistricting system is being asked to balance: total
population, voting-age population, citizen voting-age population, or an adjusted
count that corrects a specific residence-rule problem.
