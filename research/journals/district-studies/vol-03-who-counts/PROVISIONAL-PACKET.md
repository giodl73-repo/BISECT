# Provisional Packet: Who Counts?

This packet is the current human-review front door for District Studies Vol. 3.
The issue is in **provisional public preview**, not locked publication.

## Approved Strategy

The editor approved the qualitative preview path. Vol. 3 explains why
population equality depends on the selected population base, without publishing
exact national shift estimates or a final legal recommendation.

See [Editor Approval](decisions/editor-approval-qualitative-preview.md).

## Reader Promise

Population in redistricting is less settled than it looks. Prison residence,
citizen voting-age population, students, and overseas populations all change
what a map is asked to represent.
