# Who Counts?

This volume audits the population base used by redistricting systems. It asks a simple public question with difficult legal and empirical edges: when a district must contain equal population, which population should count?

## Current Posture

This issue is in provisional public preview. It uses prison gerrymandering as the reader hook, explains the main population definitions, and treats citizen-VAP and prison-adjusted claims as audited candidates rather than final legal conclusions.

Do not lock this issue as final publication until the N.1, N.3, and N.5 gates close or are formally scoped down.
