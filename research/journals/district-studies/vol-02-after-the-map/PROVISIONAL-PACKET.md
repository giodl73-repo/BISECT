# Provisional Packet: After the Map

This packet is the current human-review front door for District Studies Vol. 2.
The issue is in **provisional public preview**, not locked publication.

## Approved Strategy

The editor approved the qualitative preview path. Vol. 2 explains which
downstream outcome claims are promising and which remain fragile, without
publishing exact O.1 or O.4 values.

See [Editor Approval](decisions/editor-approval-qualitative-preview.md).

## Reader Promise

After a map is drawn, what changes for voters and representatives? This issue
auditions evidence about competitiveness, constituent access, participation,
polarization, and composite representation quality.
