# Decision: Preview, Not Locked

## Decision

Keep District Studies Vol. 2 in **provisional public preview**.

Do not mark the issue `locked` yet. The issue is suitable to show publicly as a
staged outcomes draft, but it should not be presented as final publication.

## Rationale

The qualitative preview path is coherent and safe, but the issue still carries
open source-chain gates:

- O.1 vote aggregation and single-run variance.
- O.4 routing, address, geocoding, and counterfactual office-location
  provenance.
- O.2 statistical power.
- O.3 identification.
- O.5 weighting, validation, and legal-language boundaries.

These are acceptable for a preview because exact values and strong causal/legal
claims are not published. They still argue against a final lock.

Use this label:

> locked publication.
