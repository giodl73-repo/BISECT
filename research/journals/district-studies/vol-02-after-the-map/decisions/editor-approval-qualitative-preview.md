# Editor Approval: Qualitative Preview

## Decision

Approve the qualitative preview path for District Studies Vol. 2 and move the
issue from **audition** to **provisional public preview**.

The preview may explain downstream outcome claims without publishing exact O.1
competitiveness values or exact O.4 travel-time values.

## Approved Public Shape

Vol. 2 is a staged outcomes issue. It teaches that downstream claims differ in
evidentiary strength:

- constituent distance is the cleanest measured outcome candidate;
- competitiveness is the strongest political outcome hook;
- turnout and polarization remain cautionary;
- RQI is a synthesis question, not a legal threshold.

## Locked Boundaries For Preview Drafting

- No exact O.1 competitive-district values.
- No exact O.4 travel-time values.
- No causal turnout claim.
- No causal polarization claim.
- No RQI legal-testimony language.
- No RQI threshold language.
