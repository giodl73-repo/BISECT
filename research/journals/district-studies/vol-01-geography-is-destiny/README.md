# District Studies Vol. 1: Geography Is Destiny

This issue is in **provisional public preview**. The scope-down strategy has
been approved and article drafts are coherent enough to show as staged work,
but the issue is not locked publication.

## Reader Promise

Partisan outcomes in American redistricting are strongly constrained by where
voters live. This issue tests how much geography explains before moving to
reform, adoption, or legal claims.
