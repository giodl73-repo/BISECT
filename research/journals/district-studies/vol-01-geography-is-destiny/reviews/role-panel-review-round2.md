# Role Panel Review: Draft Package

## Result

The five-piece draft package is coherent and suitable for **audition**. It is
not yet suitable for **provisional** because T.5 provenance and G.1 statistical
language still require source review.

No role recommends reviving the result-bearing measurement slot for Vol. 1
without post-fix L.1/C.5 output provenance.

## Promotion Decision

Do not promote the issue to provisional yet.

Promote only after one of these happens:

1. T.5 source requests are answered; or
2. T.5 numeric claims are explicitly removed from the first public issue and
   the formal article runs as a qualitative research note; and
3. G.1 language receives SCALE/DATUM review.
