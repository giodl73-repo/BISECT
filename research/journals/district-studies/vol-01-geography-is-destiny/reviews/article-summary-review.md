# Article Summary Review

## Review Result

The article-summary package is suitable for **audition display** and internal
editorial selection. It is not yet suitable for **provisional** because the
central quantitative gates remain open.

The summaries do the most important public-cleanup job: they make the issue's
diagnostic promise without turning it into an adoption claim, a legal claim, or
a theorem.

## Promotion Decision

Do not promote Vol. 1 to provisional yet.

Promote after one of these paths is complete:

1. **Source-chain path**: close T.5 parameter/seed/C(G) gates and G.1 exact
   language gates, while keeping L.1/C.5 measurement deferred.
2. **Scope-down path**: remove all quantitative T.5 table dependency from the
   first public issue, keep G.1 at qualitative ensemble-position language, and
   publish the issue as an explicitly diagnostic opening issue.

The scope-down path is faster and probably better for a first public issue. It
lets Vol. 1 explain the frame while later issues carry the heavier numerical
claims after the source chain is closed.
