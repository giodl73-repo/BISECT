# Provisional Packet: Geography Is Destiny

This packet is the current human-review front door for District Studies Vol. 1.
The issue is in **provisional public preview**, not locked publication.

## Approved Strategy

The editor approved the scope-down path. Vol. 1 introduces the geography-first
diagnostic frame without publishing exact T.5 table values, exact G.1
percentile claims, or L.1/C.5 result-bearing measurement claims.

See [Editor Approval](decisions/editor-approval-scope-down.md).

## Reader Promise

Partisan outcomes in American redistricting are strongly constrained by where
voters live. This issue tests that geography-first explanation before moving to
reform, adoption, or legal claims.
