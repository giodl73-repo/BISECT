# Editor Approval: Scope-Down Strategy

## Decision

Approve the scope-down strategy for District Studies Vol. 1 and move the issue
from **audition** toward **provisional public preview**.

The issue will not wait for exact T.5 table provenance, exact G.1 percentile
language, or L.1/C.5 output regeneration. Those gates remain open, but they are
out of scope for the first public version of this issue.

## Approved Public Shape

Vol. 1 is a diagnostic opening issue. It introduces the geography-first frame:
partisan outcomes can be strongly constrained by where voters live before any
claim about reform, adoption, or legal proof.

## Locked Boundaries For Provisional Drafting

- No exact T.5 table values.
- No exact G.1 percentile claims.
- No L.1/C.5 result-bearing measurement claims.
- No adoption recommendation.
- No legal conclusion.
- Treat "Geography Is Destiny" as a title, not a theorem.

## Provisional Drafting Instruction

Expand the article summaries into readable public drafts while preserving the
current claim boundaries. The first public issue should teach the diagnostic
frame, not settle the whole corpus.
