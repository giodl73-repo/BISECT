# What the Numbers Do Not Prove

This guardrail article is there to keep the issue honest. The output shows what
Bisect makes. It does not by itself prove that every output is the best map, the
only lawful map, or the final answer to every legal question.

That distinction is important. Practitioners need a readable issue, but they
also need to know what remains open after the output is shown.
