# The North Carolina Result

North Carolina is the front door because it gives readers a concrete map they
can recognize. The result should be stated in a way that ties it to the
specific run, the specific data version, and the specific output.

The public version can say the result is reproducible and visible. It should not
say that the number settles the broader legal or political debate.
