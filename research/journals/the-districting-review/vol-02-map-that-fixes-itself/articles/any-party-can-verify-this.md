# Any Party Can Verify This

Reproducibility is the most practical claim in the issue. If the same command,
data version, and manifest produce the same output, then the system is
inspectable by anyone with the files.

That is a strong practitioner story because it moves the discussion out of the
realm of trust and into the realm of record-keeping.
