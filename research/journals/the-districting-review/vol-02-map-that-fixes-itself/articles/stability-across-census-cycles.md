# Stability Across Census Cycles

Stability matters to practitioners because they need to know whether a tool's
behavior changes drastically from one census cycle to the next. The answer
should be scoped to the cycles actually tested.

This article should stay focused on consistency across 2000, 2010, and 2020 and
avoid implying that all future cycles will behave the same way.
