# Provisional Packet: The Map That Fixes Itself

This packet is the current human-review front door for The Districting Review
Vol. 2. The issue is in **provisional public preview**, not locked publication.

## Approved Strategy

The editor approved the qualitative preview path. Vol. 2 explains what Bisect
makes from public data, how a practitioner can verify it, and what the output
does not prove.

how reproducible are they, and which headline numbers survive source-chain
review?
