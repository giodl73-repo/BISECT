# How to Use the Defense in Court

The court-facing version of the defense should be modest.

The useful claim is that the baseline workflow is auditable, reproducible, and
free of partisan input. That supports a factual record. It does not force a
court to conclude that every map produced this way is constitutionally immune
from challenge.

That is the right line for commissioners and counsel. They can say what the
architecture guarantees, what the manifest proves, and what kinds of challenges
remain open.
