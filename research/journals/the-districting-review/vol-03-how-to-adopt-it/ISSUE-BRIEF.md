## Reader Promise (expanded)

Reform advocates often know the research and have seen the maps. What stops them is process: how does this actually become law or policy? This volume auditions concrete deliverables: model statute language, model ballot initiative text, court-order templates, commission protocols, and procurement specifications. Each remains draft material until legal and operational review.
