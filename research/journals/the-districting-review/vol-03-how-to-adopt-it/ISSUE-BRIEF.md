## Reader Promise (expanded)

Reform advocates often know the research and have seen the maps. What stops them is process: how does this actually become law? This volume answers that question with concrete deliverables — not proposals, but draft text. Model statute language for the DIA. Model ballot initiative text adding an algorithmic baseline to the California Prop 11/20 framework. Three model court orders. A step-by-step commission adjustment protocol. And a procurement specification a state redistricting office can use to contract for bisect implementation.
