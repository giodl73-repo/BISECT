# Source Requests

## P.0 Reform Landscape

- Keep the reform-paradox framing descriptive.
- Do not convert political economy into a legal conclusion.

## P.1 Federal Legislation

- Keep Article I, Section 4 authority precise and note non-commandeering limits.
- Separate congressional authority from state legislative authority.
- Keep the Apportionment Act analogy historical, not dispositive.

## P.2 Ballot Initiatives

- Keep model initiative text state-specific and labeled as draft.
- Separate constitutional entrenchment from federal preemption.

## P.3 Commission Integration

- Keep commission protocols operational and not mandatory for all commissions.
- Preserve documented departure language and verification steps.

## P.4 Court Remedies

- Keep the court-order templates tied to remedy contexts, not universal commands.
- Separate mandatory, special-master-input, and adequacy-standard orders.

## P.5 Costs and Procurement

- Keep cost numbers tied to stated assumptions.
- Preserve procurement language as a sample RFP, not a legal requirement.
