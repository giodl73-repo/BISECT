# Provisional Packet: How to Adopt It

This packet is the current human-review front door for The Districting Review
Vol. 3. The issue is in **provisional public preview**, not locked publication.

## Approved Strategy

The editor approved the qualitative preview path. Vol. 3 gives practitioners
draftable adoption language for federal, state, commission, and court pathways
without pretending those pathways are identical.

See [Editor Approval](decisions/editor-approval-qualitative-preview.md).

## Reader Promise

There are several adoption pathways for an algorithmic baseline -- federal
statute, state initiative, court remedy, and commission protocol. This volume
tests draft language for each.
