# How to Adopt It

This volume is the adoption-path issue. It turns the research and the output
story into concrete language for statutes, ballot initiatives, commission rules,
court orders, and procurement specs.

## Current Posture

The issue is in provisional public preview. It should be useful to legislators,
counsel, and commission chairs without implying that any one pathway is already
legally settled.

Do not write this as a single federal-answer issue. Its job is to show the
adoption pathways side by side.

## Gates

- BOUNDARY: do not convert model language into legal advice.
- WARD: keep state-law and commission-rule differences explicit.
- COVENANT: treat court orders and procurement language as reproducible drafts.
- DATUM: P.0 is framing; P.1-P.5 are the deliverables.
