## Risky-Claim Scan

The scan intentionally flags terms like "mandate," "universal," and "legal
advice" where they appear in blocking language. In the current package, those
terms are used to fence off overclaiming, not to assert a claim.

path. Do not mark Vol. 3 as locked publication.
