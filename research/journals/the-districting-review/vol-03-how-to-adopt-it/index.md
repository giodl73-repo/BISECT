# How to Adopt It

> Provisional public preview. This issue is a staged adoption draft, not a
> locked publication.

The research is not enough by itself. This issue shows how the algorithm gets
adopted through draftable language for federal law, state initiatives,
commission rules, court orders, and procurement.
