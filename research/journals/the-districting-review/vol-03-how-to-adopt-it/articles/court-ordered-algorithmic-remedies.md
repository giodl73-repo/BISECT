# Court-Ordered Algorithmic Remedies

Courts need remedy language, not theory. This article should present the three
template orders in a way that a judge or special master could actually use
without pretending the order is universal.

The useful distinction is between mandatory relief, special-master-input relief,
and adequacy-standard relief.
