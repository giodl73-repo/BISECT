# The Reform Paradox

Reform is hard because the people who can adopt it often benefit from the status
quo. That is the basic political problem this issue is trying to route around.

This framing is not the point of the volume by itself. It is the reason the
issue exists: practitioners need language that can survive the political process
instead of assuming the process is automatic.
