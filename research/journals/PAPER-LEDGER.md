# Paper Use Ledger

This ledger keeps the research press from reusing the same ordinary paper across
issues by accident. A source can move through three states:

- **audition-reserved**: currently assigned to a candidate issue but not locked.
- **preview-reserved**: assigned to a public preview but not final publication.
- **locked**: assigned to a published issue; do not reuse as an ordinary article.
- **available**: known source that has not yet been assigned.

Reference sources may still be cited across issues for context, but a locked
paper should not serve again as a headline article unless the later issue is an
explicit best-of, retrospective, correction, or response.

## Ledger Rules

1. Add a row when a paper enters an audition pool.
2. Change `audition-reserved` to `locked` only when the issue locks.
3. Change `audition-reserved` back to `available` if the paper is released from
   the issue.
4. Keep support citations distinct from article assignments.
5. Record exceptions explicitly before publishing a best-of, response, or
   retrospective issue.
